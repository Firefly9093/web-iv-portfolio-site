joy for all generations

I was asked to create a piece that would target an age group that is not normally targeted for a product of my choice. Whipped lightning was a lot of fun to work with because I was able to bring two generations together and let them have some fun. Their personalities began to show and I was able to capture this shot and create a piece that reminds the viewers you don’t have to be 21 to enjoy alcohol infused whipped toppings!
